# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Coffee enjoyers of all ages in Indonesia. They're looking for a local coffee brand that feels genuine — not another polished chain. They want bold flavors, no pretense, and a brand that speaks their language.

## Product Purpose

Kopi KHA exists to be the go-to local coffee brand for daily caffeine needs. Success means: customers recognize the brand, order regularly, and feel pride in supporting a local maker over corporate chains.

## Brand Personality

Local. Authentic. Delicious.

Voice is direct, casual, unapologetic. No corporate polish, no soft drama. Speaks like a friend who happens to make great coffee.

## Anti-references

Not Starbucks. Not artisanal third-wave coffee with tiny cups and pour-over aesthetic. Not generic modern SaaS beige. Not mainstream corporate coffee branding. The bold character already exists — preserve it, don't dilute into safe/neutral territory.

## Design Principles

1. **Bold is the brand, not a decoration.** Every visual choice should reinforce the "no soft drama" attitude — strong type, sharp contrasts, confident layout.
2. **Local first.** This isn't trying to be international. The design should feel rooted in its context, not imported from a global template.
3. **Show the product, not the process.** Coffee is about taste and ritual, not extraction methods. Lead with the experience.
4. **Direct beats clever.** Copy and UI should communicate clearly. No riddles, no forced wit.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Standard WCAG compliance. Ensure readable contrast, keyboard navigation, and reduced motion support. No specific user needs beyond baseline accessibility.
